About Our Team

We are a multidisciplinary team of students passionate about addressing food security challenges through community-driven solutions. Our diverse backgrounds enable us to approach this complex issue from multiple perspectives.

Project Objectives

Analyze the impact of community gardens on local food security
Identify best practices for sustainable urban agriculture
Develop actionable recommendations for community implementation
Create practical tools for garden planning and management
Foster community engagement through collaborative food systems
